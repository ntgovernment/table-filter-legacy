Threatened plants

This is a list of threatened plants in the Northern Territory.

Australian status indicates conservation status under the Environment Protection and Biodiversity Conservation Act 1999.

Details on species’ listing under this can be found on Species Profiles (SPRAT).

Northern Territory status indicates conservation status under the Territory Parks and Wildlife Conservation Act 1976.

The threatened plant species of the Tiwi Islands are covered by a single draft recovery plan PDF (586.3 KB).

See the list of threatened animals.
